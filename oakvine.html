What makes it different?

Oak & Vine is a wine buying experience. The user will learn about their tastes and preferences to make them a more educated shopper.

The first step is the quiz. All the user has to do is answer five questions about their likes and dislikes when it comes to foods and flavors. They are asked about if they like citrus, berries, coffee. This quiz determines the type of wine they are more likely to enjoy. At the end of the quiz, the user is presented with a full page of information including shopping reccomendations.

When the user selects a bottle of wine, the product page gives them full details about the bottle including information about the winery, flavor notes of the individual bottle, and food pairings.
