How does it work?

Tap & Go is a way for the user to explore activities in their area, based on their location.

The first step is to choose your interests from a vast list of options. The icons change color once selected.

The next step is to start exploring. Users will see a large, interactive map showing all of the activities going on in their area along with a corresponding list.

The list can always be filtered further by choosing different interests or toggling between weeks.

Once the user sees something they like, all they have to do is click on it.

The event page will show all of the details including a map of the location, photos and reviews. From this page, the user has the option to pay and reserve their spot.

Research

I wanted to make Tap & Go an app that is enjoyable for everyone to use.
I interviewed three people with three very different lifestyles to figure out what each user
would like to achieve and what their current frustrations are with current apps.
